What Is RunestoneMMO?

RunestoneMMO is a realm‑shaping MMO platform for servers seeking deeper lore, richer progression, and mechanics worthy of a true fantasy world. It is designed as a shared core that future modules can stand upon — combat, professions, villages, quests, and world content all plug into the same spine.

The first pillar, RunestoneCore, already provides multi‑currency economy, auctions, player shops, moderation, scoreboards, attributes, daily rewards, world rules, sleep logic, mob health bars, and a robust player data model — all wired through a clean subsystem lifecycle.

RunestoneMMO is a long‑term project — the forge is active, and the mountain is still being carved.

RunestoneCore — The Heart of the Mountain

RunestoneCore is the ancient engine that binds the world together. Within its runes lie the systems that shape kingdoms:

  • Multi‑currency economy with banking, taxes, exchange, treasury, and ledgers
  • Auctions and player‑forged shops with escrow, taxes, and offline delivery queues
  • Warden tools: moderation, reports, jailing, maintenance mode, audit trail, staff alerts
  • Core player profiles with balances, combat XP, titles, toggles, preferences, and daily reward state
  • Attribute and combat scaffolding: damage math, crits, biome scaling, mob attribute application
  • Custom scoreboards with conditions for combat, menus, worlds, regions, and permissions
  • Rune‑crafted menu framework and cooldown registry
  • Mob health bars and world rules (safe zones, death rules, gameplay toggles, sleep system)
  • Task and listener registries, lifecycle subsystem manager, and Adventure‑based messaging
  • Vault economy bridge for compatibility with existing plugins

RunestoneCore is the platform. The RPG layer is what comes next.

Future Modules — From the Forge

  • XP & Skills Engine — a unified RunestoneXPService for all professions and combat
  • Professions — mining, smithing, gathering, artisan crafts
  • Villages — player‑founded settlements with claiming, protections, upgrades, diplomacy, and treasuries
  • Oregen — riches beneath the stone
  • Treecapitator — fell the forest with dwarven might
  • Quest & Content Systems — quests, achievements, titles, loot tables, and world drops
  • Exploration & World Events — elites, bosses, dungeons, seasonal cycles
  • Network & Scaling — SQL storage, multi‑server sync, proxy compatibility

All social grouping will be built around Villages — there are no clans, guilds, or alliances.
