Runestone Vale

A mountain-forged fantasy MMORPG realm — built on custom systems, shaped by its community, and still being carved into something greater.

Checking server…

Runestone Vale is a Minecraft Java MMORPG realm running on a fully custom plugin suite. It is not a survival server with extras bolted on — it is a purpose-built world with its own identity, progression systems, economy, quests, NPCs, and long-term direction.

The realm is in active development. Early players, testers, and community members are part of shaping what the Vale becomes. The foundation is live and playable. The larger vision is still being forged.

The Realm of Runestone Vale

Beneath mountain shadow and carved stone, Runestone Vale is being shaped into a realm of old halls, frontier paths, rune-marked places, dangerous wilds, and forgotten depths. The tone blends dark fantasy, dwarven mountain-kingdom identity, and the ambition of a long-term MMORPG adventure.

The goal is a realm that feels grounded, ancient, and alive — not a temporary world built around resets. Every major system is being built toward one idea: making the Vale feel like a place worth returning to.

⚔️

Combat & Survival

Custom damage model, stamina system, combat levels, graves on death, and mob scaling that makes danger feel meaningful at every stage of progression.

⛏️

Skills & Professions

13 active professions — Mining, Forestry, Fishing, Smithing, Exploration, Farming, Cooking, Foraging, Hunting, Combat, and more — each with XP, milestones, and level-locked rewards.

🧙

Quests & NPCs

NPC dialogue, quest packages, objective tracking, turn-ins, shops, and questlines that make the world feel inhabited rather than empty.

🧭

Travel & Navigation

Login Hub with compass navigation, warp system, sethome, plot marketplace, and world travel — all accessible through clean in-game menus.

💰

Economy & Community

Banks, markets, auction house, daily rewards, and a Discord-linked community bridge that keeps the realm economy and player base connected.

🏰

Dungeons & World Content

Dedicated content spaces are being prepared for dungeons, boss arenas, quest rooms, raids, trials, and world events. Planned

Join the Team

Runestone Vale is actively looking for dedicated individuals to help build, moderate, and grow the realm. All roles are volunteer-based. We value reliability, creativity, and a genuine interest in the project's direction. Applications and enquiries are handled through Discord.

🏗️

Builder

Help shape the physical world of Runestone Vale. We are looking for builders with a strong sense of dark fantasy atmosphere — towns, dungeons, landmarks, and world detail.

🛡️

Moderator

Keep the community fair and welcoming. Moderators handle player reports, enforce server rules, and help maintain a positive environment across Discord and in-game.

⚙️

Administrator

Support server operations, assist with testing, help manage systems, and work closely with the development team. Admins are trusted with elevated access and responsibility.

📋

How to Apply

Join the Discord and express your interest in the relevant channel. Include your experience, what role you are applying for, and why you want to be part of the project.
