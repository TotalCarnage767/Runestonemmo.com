Welcome to the Vale

Beneath ancient mountains and forgotten halls, the land of Runestone Vale endures beneath old runes, buried history, and a growing age of discovery. Travelers, builders, traders, and adventurers arrive not to follow a disposable world, but to become part of one.

The goal of Runestone Vale is to feel like a true fantasy realm — a world where progression, economy, professions, exploration, territory, and community all belong to the same living setting instead of feeling like disconnected plugin features stitched together.

Whether you delve into the mountain for rare ores, rise through gathering and crafting professions, carve out land, build wealth, or simply search for your place in the realm, your path becomes part of the ongoing history of the Vale.

Realm Features

Persistent Fantasy World

  • A custom medieval-fantasy MMORPG identity
  • Designed for long-term progression and world-building
  • A player-facing realm instead of a basic short-lived survival server

Skills & Professions

  • Mining, Forestry, Fishing, Exploration, Smithing, and more
  • Profession-based progression built through RunestoneSkills
  • Custom systems designed to feel rewarding and expandable

Economy & Trade

  • Multi-currency economy foundation
  • Auctions, shops, banks, exchange, treasury, and ledgers
  • Trade-driven progression that supports a living realm

Claims & Settlements

  • Land protections and trusted access systems
  • Towns and territory direction for organized expansion
  • Built to support a realm shaped by its players

Custom Ores & Progression

  • Runestone Ore and Firestone Ore integrated into Mining progression
  • Raw ore drops and ingot progression variants
  • Mining and Smithing designed to feel connected instead of separate

Immersive Presentation

  • Custom scoreboards, HUD layers, and player-facing UI
  • Mob health bars and fantasy-themed presentation
  • A world built to feel cohesive, stylized, and memorable

Powered by RunestoneMMO

Runestone Vale is the live world. RunestoneMMO is the engine forging it.

The foundation begins with RunestoneCore, which provides the deeper systems that hold the realm together: economy, player data, scoreboards, travel systems, moderation, world rules, claims, towns, and core MMO scaffolding.

It is then expanded by RunestoneSkills, the progression layer bringing professions, gathering, crafting, exploration, and long-term skill advancement into the world.

Runestone Vale is where those systems are tested, balanced, lived in, and shaped into a real playable realm.

State of the Forge

The forges are active. Runestone Vale continues to grow as the flagship realm while the wider RunestoneMMO ecosystem expands around it. Economy systems, progression systems, professions, claims, territories, world presentation, and custom content are all being shaped into a more complete MMORPG experience.

Current development is focused on deepening the world through skills, custom ore generation, settlements, server usability improvements, Citizens / NPC bridge features, and future content that makes the realm feel increasingly alive.

The mountain is still being carved — and the Vale is only getting stronger.

Why Start Here?

If you are discovering the project for the first time, Runestone Vale is the best place to begin. It is the player-facing home of the wider vision — the place where custom systems stop being ideas on paper and become part of a living world.

For players, it is the gateway into the realm. For followers of the project, it is the clearest look at what RunestoneMMO is becoming.
