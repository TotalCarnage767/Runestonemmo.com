What Is RunestoneMMO?

RunestoneMMO is a realm-shaping MMO platform for servers seeking deeper lore, richer progression, and mechanics worthy of a true fantasy world. It is designed as a shared core that future modules can stand upon — combat, skills, settlements, quests, and world content all plug into the same spine.

The first pillar, RunestoneCore, provides multi-currency economy, auctions, player shops, moderation, scoreboards, attributes, daily rewards, world rules, sleep logic, mob health bars, and a robust player data model — all wired through a clean subsystem lifecycle.

The second pillar, RunestoneSkills, brings MMO progression to gathering, crafting, and discovery — built as a module that integrates into the same curve logic and APIs used by combat.

RunestoneMMO is a long-term project — the forge is active, and the mountain is still being carved.

RunestoneCore — The Heart of the Mountain

RunestoneCore is the ancient engine that binds the world together. Within its runes lie the systems that shape kingdoms:

  • Multi-currency economy with banking, taxes, exchange, treasury, and ledgers
  • Auctions and player-forged shops with escrow, taxes, and offline delivery queues
  • Warden tools: moderation, reports, jailing, maintenance mode, audit trail, staff alerts
  • Core player profiles with balances, combat XP, titles, toggles, preferences, and daily reward state
  • Attribute and combat scaffolding: damage math, crits, scaling hooks, mob attribute application
  • Custom scoreboards with conditions for combat, menus, worlds, regions, and permissions
  • Rune-crafted menu framework and cooldown registry
  • Mob health bars and world rules (safe zones, death rules, gameplay toggles, sleep system)
  • Land claims and protections (containers, block rules, trust lists, flags)
  • Towns / Territories v1 (membership, ranks, treasury, claim rules, protection listeners)
  • Task and listener registries, lifecycle subsystem manager, and Adventure-based messaging
  • Vault economy bridge for compatibility with existing plugins

RunestoneCore is the platform. The RPG layer is what comes next.

RunestoneSkills — The Crafts of the Deep Halls

RunestoneSkills is the progression layer built on top of RunestoneCore — designed for MMO-style servers that want gathering and artisan growth tied into the same curve and level rules as combat.

Every skill is tuned through its own YAML file and built with anti-exploit safeguards (cooldowns, rate limits, natural-only rules, and validation checks where required).

  • Mining — block XP tables and tool validation (config-driven)
  • Forestry — natural tree cutting (logs/woods/stems/hyphae) with axe validation
  • Fishing — catch-based progression with configurable rules
  • Hunting — passive mob progression with filters and tuning
  • Farming — crop and plant harvesting progression (tuneable)
  • Cooking — food crafting/cooking progression (tuneable)
  • Smithing — weapons, tools, armor, smithing upgrades, and smelting progression
  • Exploration — background travel XP, biome discovery XP, landmarks framework
  • Foraging — wild vegetation gathering and berry harvesting (natural-only)

RunestoneSkills is live — and the balancing pass is still underway.
