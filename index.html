Runestone Vale is a medieval fantasy survival realm shaped by rune-forged stonework, long-term progression, exploration, economy, quests, NPCs, and a world identity built to feel connected.

The Vale is not just a survival map with plugins added on top. It is being built as a living MMORPG-style realm — where skills, travel, markets, combat, dungeons, towns, and player progress are meant to grow together over time.

This world is still being forged. Early players, testers, builders, explorers, and community members are helping shape what Runestone Vale becomes.

The Realm of the Vale

Beneath mountain shadow and carved stone, Runestone Vale is being shaped into a realm of old halls, frontier paths, rune-marked places, dangerous wilds, settlements, and forgotten depths waiting to be uncovered.

The tone of the world blends medieval fantasy, dwarven mountain-kingdom identity, and the broader feeling of a long-term MMORPG adventure. The goal is a realm that feels grounded, ancient, and alive — not a temporary world built around resets.

Every major system is being built toward one idea: making the Vale feel like a place worth returning to.

Systems Powering the Realm

Runestone Vale is powered by the custom RunestoneMMO plugin suite, supported by trusted server tools for protection, assets, compatibility, permissions, NPCs, and world building.

RunestoneMMO Plugins

  • RunestoneCore — shared server foundation, economy, menus, travel, world rules, data, and core systems
  • RunestoneSkills — 13 professions, skill XP, gathering progression, crafting direction, and long-term advancement
  • RunestoneCombat — custom combat direction, attributes, scaling, and combat progression
  • RunestoneStamina — stamina, movement pacing, survival pressure, and future ability-cost support
  • RunestoneHub — player hub, travel access, market access, bank access, quest info, and server updates
  • RunestoneNPCs — NPC bindings, dialogue, quests, shops, and world interaction systems
  • RunestoneAdminTool — staff tools, admin menus, server management, and controlled world access

Supporting Server Plugins

  • Oraxen — custom items, blocks, models, and resource-pack identity
  • Citizens — NPC framework for characters, shops, quests, and world interaction
  • WorldGuard — region protection and controlled areas
  • WorldEdit — building, shaping, schematic work, and world creation support
  • LuckPerms — roles, permissions, and staff access control
  • Vault — economy compatibility layer
  • PlaceholderAPI — display placeholders for menus, scoreboards, and integrations
  • Geyser & Floodgate — Bedrock access support
  • ViaVersion suite — version compatibility support
