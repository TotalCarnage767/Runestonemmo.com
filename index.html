What Is RunestoneMMO?

RunestoneMMO is a realm‑shaping RPG system for servers seeking deeper lore, richer progression, and mechanics worthy of a true fantasy world.

It brings forth currencies, world laws, auctions, shops, attributes, scoreboards, and more — all powered by the mighty RunestoneCore.

RunestoneCore — The Heart of the Mountain

RunestoneCore is the ancient engine that binds the world together. Within its runes lie the systems that shape kingdoms:

  • Multi‑currency economy of gold, shards, and treasures
  • Auctions & player‑forged shops
  • Warden tools: banishment, muting, jailing, vanishing
  • Player attributes, profiles, and heroic statistics
  • Custom scoreboards of glory and wealth
  • Rune‑crafted menu interfaces
  • Cooldowns and timed rituals
  • Mob health bars — the sight of battle
  • World laws & realm‑wide decrees
  • If it's configurable, it's tuneable
