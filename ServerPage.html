The Tale of the Vale

Long before the first adventurer set foot upon its soil, the land now called Runestone Vale was a quiet frontier — a place where ancient runes slept beneath moss and stone, waiting for a new age to awaken them. When the Runestone stirred, so too did the world: forests shifted, ore veins pulsed with forgotten magic, and the spirits of old whispered once more through the mountains.

Now, the Vale stands at the dawn of a new era. Travelers arrive from distant realms, drawn by the promise of discovery, mastery, and the chance to carve their name into the living history of a world shaped by the Runestone itself. Whether you rise as a miner, a forester, a smith, an explorer, or a defender of the wilds, your journey becomes part of the Vale’s story.

What Is Runestone Vale?

Runestone Vale is the flagship world of the RunestoneMMO project — a persistent, lore-rich RPG server built entirely on top of RunestoneCore, the custom engine that powers every system, progression loop, and interaction.

Every mechanic is handcrafted, every progression path intentional, and every system designed to support long-term MMO-style play. The Vale is not a generic SMP — it is a curated, evolving world where your character grows, your choices matter, and your legacy endures.
