Server Features

What makes Runestone Vale more than a basic survival server.

Runestone Vale is built as a custom fantasy MMORPG-style realm where progression, economy, exploration, professions, custom content, and server systems are designed to work together as one world.

Instead of feeling like a loose collection of plugins and commands, the goal is to make the realm feel cohesive — a place where systems support the world, and the world supports long-term player progression.

Skills & Professions

  • Mining, Forestry, Fishing, Exploration, Smithing, and more
  • Profession-based progression built through RunestoneSkills
  • Custom systems designed to feel rewarding and expandable

Economy & Trade

  • Multi-currency economy foundation

  • Trade-driven progression that supports a living realm

Claims & Settlements

  • Land protections and trusted access systems
  • Towns and territory direction for organized expansion

If you want a fantasy survival realm with long-term direction, custom progression, and a stronger sense of identity, this is where the journey begins.
