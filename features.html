Server Features

Runestone Vale is a Paper 1.21.1 fantasy MMORPG survival realm built around progression, quests, economy, custom content, and long-term world identity.

Server at a Glance

Runestone Vale is a fantasy MMORPG survival server with a medieval, dwarven, and rune-forged identity. It is built around long-term progression, economy, quests, NPCs, custom content, exploration, and systems that feel connected rather than scattered.

Platform

  • Paper 1.21.1
  • Java Edition — primary support
  • Bedrock access via Geyser + Floodgate
  • Multi-version support via ViaVersion suite

Realm Identity

  • Dark medieval fantasy atmosphere
  • Dwarven and rune-forged aesthetic
  • Long-term character and skill progression
  • Connected economy, quests, travel, and combat

Powered By

  • Custom RunestoneMMO plugin suite
  • Oraxen — custom items, blocks, and assets
  • Citizens — NPC framework
  • WorldGuard, WorldEdit, LuckPerms, Vault

Current Focus

  • Follower / companion system
  • Mining progression overhaul
  • Dungeon and content-world preparation
  • Deeper NPC quest content

Core Features

Starter Experience

New players receive a curated starter kit, a written realm manual, and a Hub Compass to guide their first steps in the Vale. Onboarding is designed to feel like entering a world, not a tutorial.

Hub Compass

A central in-game menu that replaces most commands. Players access travel, economy, bank, quests, skills, daily rewards, mailbox, and graves — all from a single item without memorising commands.

Skills & Progression

Thirteen professions — Mining, Forestry, Fishing, Farming, Cooking, Smithing, Hunting, Foraging, Exploration, Strength, Defense, Range, and more — each with XP, milestones, and level-locked rewards.

Quests & NPCs

NPC characters offer quests, dialogue, and shops. Quest state persists across sessions and dialogue adapts to where a player is in a questline. More quest content is actively being added.

Combat System

Custom combat framework with attribute-based scaling, typed damage, and combat progression. Mobs are tuned to feel meaningful at different stages of character development.

Stamina & Survival

A stamina layer adds pacing and survival pressure to movement and actions. Progression reduces stamina costs over time. Stamina brews provide short-term recovery options.

Economy & Market

Multi-currency economy with wallet and bank systems, player shops, auction house, and market tools. All accessible through the Hub Compass without commands.

Travel & Teleport

Spawn, home, back, and teleport request tools are available through the Hub. Travel is designed to feel purposeful — the world rewards exploration rather than instant access everywhere.

Graves & Death Recovery

Graves preserve player items on death. Players can view active graves, death locations, and time remaining through the Hub Compass.

Custom Assets

Oraxen-powered custom items, blocks, models, and textures give the realm its visual identity. Custom skill blocks, stamina brews, and ore assets are part of the resource pack.

Daily Rewards & Mail

Daily claim system with streak tracking, and a mailbox for receiving items, currency, and messages. Both are accessible through the Hub Compass.

Claims & Territory

Personal land claims protect player builds. Town and territory systems are part of the long-term direction, with settlements and upgrades planned for future development.

Player Utilities

Most day-to-day actions are accessible through the Hub Compass in-game, reducing the need to memorise commands. Common typed commands are available for players who prefer direct access.

Hub & Navigation

  • /hub — open the Login Hub
  • /skills — view skill levels
  • /quests — view active quest journal
  • /scoreboard — scoreboard controls

Travel

  • /spawn — return to server spawn
  • /home / /sethome — home management
  • /back — return to previous location
  • /tpa <player> — teleport request

Economy

  • /balance — view wallet balance
  • /bank balance — view bank balance
  • /pay <player> <amount> — pay another player
  • /ah — auction house

Daily & Recovery

  • /daily claim — claim daily reward
  • /mail — open mailbox
  • /grave — view grave status
  • /playtime — view total playtime

Social

  • /party create — create a party
  • /party invite <player> — invite a player
  • /channel <global|local|party> — switch chat channel
  • /sort inventory — sort your inventory

Claims

  • /claim create — create a land claim
  • /claim info — inspect a claim
  • /claim trust — manage claim trust
  • /claim list — list your claims

What Is Still Growing

Runestone Vale already has a strong custom foundation, but the server is actively growing into a deeper MMORPG realm. The systems below are either live foundations being expanded, or major future directions still in development.

More Quest Content

More NPC stories, questlines, daily quests, and guided progression are planned as the world grows.

Dungeons & Bosses

A managed content world is ready for future dungeon rooms, boss arenas, raids, trials, and custom events.

Followers & Companions

NPC companions that fight alongside players, level their own skills, and can be geared and customised.

Towns & Settlements

Deeper settlement gameplay with upgrades, player identity, and territory value planned over time.

Economy Loops

More connections between skills, markets, NPC shops, quests, crafting, and rewards are planned.

Custom Content

More custom items, blocks, progression rewards, and realm-specific visuals will continue to expand the Vale.

Join the Realm

Runestone Vale is actively growing. Testers, builders, long-term players, and fantasy MMORPG fans are all welcome to help shape what the world becomes.
