Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Welcome to Runestone Vale. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (runestonemmo.com), use the Runestone Vale mobile application, or play on the Runestone Vale Minecraft server.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site, app, or server.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect includes:

Personal Data

  • Discord Information: When you log into our website or app using Discord OAuth, we collect your Discord User ID, username, and avatar URL. We do not collect your email address or password.
  • Minecraft Information: When you link your account using the in-game /rlink command, we collect your Minecraft UUID and username.

App and Server Activity Data

  • Gameplay Stats: We collect and store your in-game progression, including skill levels, combat stats, currency balances, and playtime.
  • User-Generated Content: We store chat messages sent in-game and via the app, bug reports, and community votes.
  • Diagnostic Data: We may collect crash logs and performance data from the mobile app to help us identify and fix bugs.

2. How We Use Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you to:

  • Create and manage your account across the website, app, and game server.
  • Sync your in-game character stats (skills, economy, claims) to your mobile app dashboard.
  • Enable cross-platform communication (in-app chat synced with in-game chat).
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience.
  • Prevent fraudulent transactions, monitor against theft, and protect against criminal activity.
  • Respond to customer service requests and support needs.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners and trusted affiliates.

We may disclose your information where we are legally required to do so in order to comply with applicable law, governmental requests, a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process.

4. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. All data transmitted between the app/website and our databases (Supabase) is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.

5. Account and Data Deletion

How to Request Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time. This complies with Google Play Data Safety requirements and general privacy best practices.

What gets deleted:

  • Your Discord OAuth link and associated Discord ID
  • Your Minecraft UUID link
  • Your app account profile

What is retained:

  • In-game character data (skills, claims, economy) remains on the Minecraft server unless specifically requested to be wiped by a server administrator.
  • Chat logs and moderation history may be retained for server safety and rule enforcement purposes.

To request deletion, please do one of the following:

  1. Via Discord: Open a support ticket in the official Runestone Vale Discord server and request an "App Account Deletion".
  2. Via Email: Contact our support team at support@runestonemmo.com with your Discord username and Minecraft IGN.

We will process your deletion request within 7-14 business days.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our official Discord server or through the Bug Report & Contact page on this website.
