Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to RunestoneMMO and the Runestone Vale Companion App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

When you log into the Runestone Vale Companion App using Discord OAuth, we collect only the minimum information required to authenticate you and link your app profile to your server progression. This includes:

  • Your Discord User ID
  • Your Discord Username
  • Your Discord Avatar (profile picture)

We do not collect your password, and we do not have the ability to read your messages or post on your behalf. Authentication is handled securely through Supabase and Discord's official OAuth system.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity within the companion app
  • To display your Discord username and avatar on your profile page
  • To link your Discord identity to your in-game character progression
  • To verify your role (e.g., Player, Staff, Admin) for role-specific app features

4. Data Storage and Security

Your authentication data is securely stored using Supabase, a leading open-source database provider. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. Your Discord access tokens are stored securely and are only used for authentication purposes.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is used exclusively within the RunestoneMMO ecosystem. We will only disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as you maintain an active account with us. If you wish to have your data deleted, you can log out of the app and revoke the RunestoneMMO authorization from your Discord settings (User Settings > Authorized Apps). You may also contact us to request full deletion of your profile data from our database.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
support@runestonemmo.com or via our official Discord server.
