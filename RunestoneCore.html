What RunestoneCore Is

RunestoneCore is not just another utility plugin. It is the shared foundation for a fantasy MMORPG-style server ecosystem. It handles the deeper systems that must work together cleanly if a realm is going to feel believable, stable, and expandable.

Economy, moderation, player data, UI, world rules, claims, territory systems, and progression scaffolding all live here so that future modules do not feel bolted on. The goal is to create a true core — one foundation strong enough to support professions, combat, towns, quests, exploration systems, and long-term content growth.

In simple terms: Runestone Vale is the world players enter, and RunestoneCore is the engine making that world function.

Core Systems

Economy & Trade

  • Multi-currency economy foundation
  • Banking, exchange, treasury, and ledgers
  • Auctions, player shops, and economy routing
  • Vault bridge compatibility for server ecosystems

Player Data & Profiles

  • Persistent player data and account state
  • Balances, progression scaffolding, and profile systems
  • Titles, toggles, preferences, and long-term identity
  • Designed for future expansion instead of disposable save logic

Claims & Settlements

  • Land claims and protection systems
  • Trust controls, block rules, and access management
  • Towns / territories direction with treasury and membership structure
  • Built to support player-driven settlement growth

UI & Presentation

  • Custom scoreboards and player-facing UI layers
  • Action bar / boss bar style presentation support
  • Mob health bars and immersive realm-facing display systems
  • Menu framework and shared interface utilities

Travel & Utility Systems

  • Home, teleport, and travel utility systems
  • Command aliases for faster everyday usage
  • Shared command registration and cleaner player flow
  • Infrastructure meant to reduce friction for both players and staff

Moderation & Administration

  • Staff-facing control systems and server safeguards
  • Maintenance direction, alerts, and operational tools
  • Framework for reports, enforcement, and management expansion
  • Built for long-term realm stewardship, not short-term patchwork

Why It Matters

Most servers pile together unrelated plugins and hope the result feels complete. RunestoneCore exists to do the opposite. It is meant to be the common language between systems — the part that makes economy, travel, UI, moderation, claims, and future progression mechanics feel like pieces of the same world.

That matters for players because a realm feels better when its systems agree with each other. It matters for development because building new modules becomes cleaner, safer, and more consistent over time.

RunestoneCore is the part of the project that lets RunestoneMMO grow without collapsing under its own weight.

Built for the Wider Ecosystem

RunestoneCore is the first pillar of the wider RunestoneMMO vision. It is built to support a growing family of modules, not just a single isolated feature set. RunestoneSkills already expands the progression side of that vision, and future content can stand on the same foundation without feeling disconnected.

The long-term aim is a deeper fantasy MMORPG platform — one where economy, professions, territory, NPC systems, quest content, exploration, and world-building all belong to the same forged structure.

With Runestone Vale now leading the front of the site, RunestoneCore stands here as the deeper foundation beneath it — the engine room behind the world players see.
