RunestoneSkills

The professions and progression plugin of RunestoneMMO — built to make gathering, crafting, exploration, and long-term skill growth feel like part of a living realm.

RunestoneSkills is the official professions and progression addon of the wider RunestoneMMO ecosystem. It is the system layer that turns gathering, crafting, survival work, and long-term advancement into meaningful parts of the world instead of leaving them as shallow background mechanics.

Built on top of RunestoneCore, RunestoneSkills is designed to make progression feel connected to the realm itself. Skills are not meant to exist as detached XP bars or isolated jobs — they are meant to feed the economy, resource flow, world identity, player goals, and the long-term life of Runestone Vale.

In simple terms: RunestoneCore is the foundation beneath the realm, and RunestoneSkills is the progression layer that gives the realm a living craft, gathering, and advancement identity.

What RunestoneSkills Is

RunestoneSkills is not just an XP addon. It is the professions and progression layer of the RunestoneMMO platform. Its job is to make player effort in the world feel meaningful over time — whether that effort comes through Mining, Smithing, Forestry, Fishing, Exploration, Farming, or other active profession paths.

The goal is not to create random disconnected jobs. The goal is to create a world where professions reinforce one another. Mining supports Smithing. Forestry supports material flow and crafting direction. Fishing offers a different kind of non-combat progression. Exploration rewards movement and world engagement. Farming, Cooking, Foraging, and Hunting widen the identity of how players live in the realm.

RunestoneSkills exists to make progression feel like part of the culture of the world, not just numbers hidden behind it.

Main Design Goals

Connected Progression

  • Skills are meant to support one another instead of existing in isolation
  • Gathering, crafting, refining, and exploration all reinforce the larger realm
  • Built for a cohesive MMORPG-style progression experience

Long-Term Realm Identity

  • Designed for a persistent world, not a throwaway progression loop
  • Built to support trade, travel, crafting value, and player specialization
  • Focused on strengthening the fantasy MMORPG identity of the server

Config-Driven Tuning

  • Profession pacing can be balanced per skill
  • XP flow and behavior can be tuned independently
  • Built for long-term admin control instead of hardcoded rigidity

Anti-Exploit Direction

  • Natural-only logic where appropriate
  • Placed-block and placed-resource protections where needed
  • Validation-first handling to protect progression value over time

Why RunestoneSkills Matters
