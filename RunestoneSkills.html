What RunestoneSkills Is

RunestoneSkills is the progression layer of the RunestoneMMO ecosystem. It is designed to give players a stronger sense of long-term growth through professions, resource gathering, crafting, and exploration.

Rather than treating each skill like a separate minigame, the goal is to make them feel like pieces of the same world. Mining feeds resources into crafting. Exploration supports discovery. Forestry and Fishing strengthen the economy. Smithing turns gathered materials into meaningful advancement.

In short, RunestoneSkills exists to make player progression feel like part of the realm itself.

Live Professions in the Realm

RunestoneSkills is focused on making professions feel meaningful, immersive, and connected to the wider world. These skills are part of the living identity of Runestone Vale rather than just a list of XP bars.

Mining

  • Gather valuable resources through profession-based mining progression
  • Custom ore direction including Runestone Ore and Firestone Ore
  • Supports raw ore drops and ingot progression variants
  • Mining is designed to connect directly into the wider crafting pipeline

Smithing

  • Transforms gathered materials into meaningful crafting progression
  • Built to support tools, equipment, and long-term resource value
  • Intended to feel like the natural companion to Mining
  • Part of the deeper fantasy progression identity of the realm

Forestry

  • Profession-based woodcutting and tree gathering progression
  • Natural-tree focused logic with anti-abuse safeguards
  • Built to reward gathering without encouraging cheesy farming loops
  • Supports the realm’s economy, crafting, and future expansion

Fishing

  • Catch-based progression designed around the life of the realm
  • Supports resource variety and profession diversity
  • Built to give non-combat players another meaningful path forward
  • Useful for long-term economy and world immersion

Exploration

  • Rewards movement, travel, and the experience of discovering the world
  • Supports the feeling of a large realm worth journeying through
  • Encourages players to interact with the world beyond static grinding
  • Designed to make wandering the realm feel valuable

Future Profession Growth

Configuration & Tuning

RunestoneSkills is designed so each major profession can be balanced independently. That allows the realm to tune pacing, XP flow, and resource progression without shoving every skill into one bloated config file.

Skill Config Files

  • mining.yml
  • forestry.yml
  • fishing.yml
  • exploration.yml
  • smithing.yml
  • And future files for future professions

Admin Control

  • XP values by action, material, or source
  • Per-skill pacing and progression tuning
  • Validation rules and anti-exploit behavior
  • Enable / disable control for supported systems

Workflow

  • Configs generate into plugins/RunestoneSkills/
  • Balance each skill independently without breaking the full module
  • Use restarts or reload workflows carefully for major adjustments
  • Built to support long-term admin tuning as the realm evolves

Why It Matters

A realm feels stronger when progression has identity. RunestoneSkills gives players more reasons to gather, craft, trade, explore, and stay invested over time. It helps turn the world into a place where effort matters and professions feel like part of the world’s culture instead of simple background mechanics.

It also matters for the wider project, because a deeper skills layer makes every other system stronger: economy, custom resources, future quests, world exploration, and long-term realm growth all benefit from a better profession foundation.
